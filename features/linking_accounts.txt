Linking accounts:
- Can link Battle.net account to Workshop.codes
- Can link Discord account to Workshop.codes
- Can link an OAuth account to another OAuth account
- Cannot link an account that is already linked to another account

Unlinking accounts:
- Can unlink a Battle.net account
- Can unlink a Discord account

Logging in:
- Logging in with a linked OAuth account logs into the central account
- Logging in with a recently unlinked OAuth account creates a new, unlinked account
